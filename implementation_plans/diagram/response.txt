-   **Bronze Layer**: Raw ingestion of local (2022) and web-scraped (2023-2024) datasets.
-   **Silver Layer**: Sanitized and normalized views for students and teachers, including the SUE institution mapping.
-   **Gold Layer**: Final analytical tables calculating the **Student-to-Teacher Ratio** and identifying state institutions, optimized for BI consumption.
